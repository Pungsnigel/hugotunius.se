About me

I am a Swedish programmer from Stockholm. I work as a developer at Fishbrain. Previously I've studied Computer Science at Gothenburg University, during my time in Gothenburg I also worked at Chalmers Teknologkonsulter as an IT Consultant and during the fall of 2013 I held the position of Business Area Manager for the IT department

My endless interest in all things computer and programming related has found me working with a wide range of technologies, tasks and languages. From GPGPU to web design from server administration to hardware a true jack of all trades. The challenges of programming and the rewards of expanding my knowledge keeps me tethered to computers.
